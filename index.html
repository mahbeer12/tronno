Got Questions?

FAQ

Do you sell used tires?

Yes — used tires are our specialty. We stock a wide range of sizes and inspect every tire before it's sold. It's the most affordable way to get back on the road safely.

How long does a tire changeover take?

Most changeovers are done within 30–60 minutes depending on how busy we are. Call ahead and we'll give you a realistic time estimate.

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome! For larger jobs or if you want to guarantee a time slot, it's always a good idea to call us first at (416) 786-3651.

What areas do you serve?

We're located in Scarborough on Lawrence Ave East and serve the entire east Toronto area — Scarborough, North York, Markham, and beyond.

Can you repair a flat tire?

Absolutely. Bring in your flat and we'll assess whether it can be patched or needs to be replaced — and we'll give you an honest answer either way.

Do you offer rim services?

Yes, we handle rim mounting, removal, and inspection. Whether you're switching to winter rims or need a new set mounted, we've got you covered.
